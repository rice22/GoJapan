Canal City Hakata (キャナルシティ博多) is a sprawling shopping and entertainment complex located in Fukuoka's Hakata Ward. Nicknamed a "city within the city," it seamlessly integrates over 250 shops, diverse dining options, entertainment venues, and accommodations, all centered around a picturesque artificial canal. Since its opening in 1996, Canal City has become one of Kyushu’s most visited attractions, drawing millions annually with its unique architecture and dynamic atmosphere.

Visiting Canal City

Canal City Hakata, located in Fukuoka, is a stunning blend of modern architecture and cultural charm. Known as a “city within a city,” it features a shopping complex, entertainment zones, art displays, and a central canal that flows through the middle. Whether you're here to shop, dine, or simply soak in the ambiance, Canal City offers an immersive experience unlike any other in Japan.

A view from the upper terraces where fountains perform every hour to music.

Practical Information

  • Opening Hours:
  • Shops: 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM
  • Restaurants: 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM (hours may vary)
  • Ramen Stadium: 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM
  • Cinema and Theater: Varies by schedule
  • Admission: Free to enter; pay only for what you shop, eat, or see
  • Access:
  • 10-minute walk from Hakata Station (JR, Shinkansen, and subway lines)
  • 10-15 minutes from Tenjin Station via Nishitetsu bus or subway
  • Easily accessible by foot from central Fukuoka districts

Canal City Travel Tips

Shopping & Services

  • Tax-Free Shopping: Most stores participate in Japan's tax-free program for foreign visitors—remember to show your passport at eligible stores for an 8–10% tax exemption on qualifying purchases.
  • Canal City OPA & Grand Hyatt Fukuoka: The complex includes specialty boutiques and luxury outlets inside Canal City OPA, and it’s directly connected to the upscale Grand Hyatt hotel.
  • Local Goods & Regional Souvenirs: Discover Fukuoka-exclusive items such as mentaiko (spicy cod roe), Yame green tea, and Hakata textiles.
  • Currency Exchange & ATM Services: Several international ATMs are located throughout the facility, including Seven Bank and Japan Post ATMs compatible with foreign cards.
  • Delivery Service: Some stores offer same-day luggage or package delivery to hotels within the city (check at the information desk).

Family & Amenities

  • Stroller Rentals: Available at the Information Center (limited quantity; first come, first served).
  • Parent-Child Rest Areas: Located on multiple floors, offering private nursing booths, microwave ovens, and sinks.
  • Baby Food and Diapers for Sale: Convenience stores inside the complex (like Lawson and FamilyMart) stock child essentials.
  • Free Wi-Fi: Available throughout the mall; connect to “Canalcity_Free_Wi-Fi.”
  • Barrier-Free Access: Elevators, escalators, and accessible restrooms are widely available for visitors with mobility needs.

Visitor Information & Tips

  • Multilingual Support: Signs and maps available in English, Chinese, and Korean. Many staff at the information desks speak basic English.
  • Free Lockers & Paid Luggage Storage: Coin lockers in multiple sizes available on lower floors and near entrances. Oversized luggage storage available near the Bus Center.
