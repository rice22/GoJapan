Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium (沖縄美ら海水族館, Okinawa Churaumi Suizokukan) is a world-renowned aquarium located in Motobu Town, in the northern part of Okinawa Island. It is the centerpiece of Ocean Expo Park and one of Japan’s most popular attractions outside of the mainland. “Churaumi” combines the Okinawan word for “beautiful” (chura) and “ocean” (umi), a fitting name for this extraordinary facility that showcases the marine biodiversity of the Kuroshio Current and the surrounding Pacific Ocean.

Visiting Churaumi Aquarium

Nestled within the Ocean Expo Park, is a breathtaking fusion of marine wonder and island culture. Celebrated for its massive Kuroshio Sea tank and diverse sea life—including whale sharks and manta rays—it brings visitors face-to-face with the mysteries of the deep. From hands-on tide pools to open-air dolphin shows, Churaumi offers an unforgettable journey through Okinawa’s vibrant marine ecosystems and conservation efforts.

Practical Information

  • Opening Hours:
  • March to September: 8:30 AM – 6:30 PM (last entry at 5:30 PM)
  • October to February: 8:30 AM – 6:00 PM (last entry at 5:00 PM)
  • Admission:
  • Adults: ¥2,180
  • Elementary/Junior High Students: ¥710
  • Children under 6: Free
  • Discounted tickets available after 4 PM (“Twilight Tickets”)
  • Address: 424 Ishikawa, Motobu-cho, Kunigami-gun, Okinawa 905-0206, Japan
  • Access:
  • From Naha Airport: Approximately 2 hours by car via Okinawa Expressway
  • By Bus: Direct highway buses (Yanbaru Express or Airport Limousine Bus) are available from Naha to the aquarium.

Churaumi Aquarium Travel Tips

Timing & Planning

  • Arrive Early: Beat the crowds by arriving shortly after opening—especially during weekends or holidays.
  • Plan for Half a Day: Allocate at least 3–4 hours to enjoy all exhibits, gardens, and surrounding attractions.
  • Check Event Schedules: Look ahead for feeding times, dolphin shows, and seasonal events to time your visit perfectly.
  • Off-Season Advantage: Visiting in late fall or early spring avoids crowds and offers pleasant weather.

Access & Navigation

  • Rent a Car: Public transport is limited—renting a car makes it easier to reach the aquarium and explore the area.
  • Free Parking: The aquarium has large, free parking lots just a short walk from the entrance.
  • Use the North Gate: If it’s busy, try entering via the North Gate for a quieter start.
  • Combine with Nearby Sights: Plan visits to nearby attractions like Emerald Beach, Ocean Expo Park, or Bise Fukugi Tree Road.

Visitor Information & Tips

  • Bring Sunscreen & Water: Okinawa’s sun can be strong—stay hydrated and protected, especially in outdoor areas.
  • Wear Comfy Shoes: You’ll be walking through large indoor and outdoor sections, including sloped paths.
  • Pack Light Snacks: Outside food isn’t allowed inside, but you can picnic at nearby parks or rest zones.
  • Use Coin Lockers: Stash bulky items in the aquarium’s coin lockers for a hands-free visit.
