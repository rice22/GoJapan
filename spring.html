üéØ Spring Trivia

What is the Japanese term for cherry blossom viewing?

Hanami

Literally meaning "flower viewing"

How long do cherry blossoms typically bloom?

1-2 weeks

Peak bloom lasts only a few days

Which city is famous for the Philosopher's Path?

Kyoto

A peaceful walking path lined with hundreds of cherry trees

What do people traditionally eat during hanami?

Bento & Sake

Picnic boxes and traditional rice wine

When does cherry blossom season start in Tokyo?

Late March

Usually around March 20-25

How many varieties of cherry trees are there in Japan?

Over 200

Somei Yoshino is the most common

What is the cherry blossom forecast called?

Sakura Zensen

Cherry blossom front - tracks bloom progression

What does sakura symbolize in Japanese culture?

Life's Beauty

The fleeting nature of life and beauty
