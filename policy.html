Privacy & Cookies Policy

Welcome to Go Japan. Your privacy and trust are very important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data and how we use cookies.

Cookies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us provide a better browsing experience. On this site, we use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze how visitors use the site to improve content and features
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations related to traveling in Japan

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that some parts of the site may not function properly without cookies.

Data We Collect

We may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited to help us understand visitor behavior and improve our travel guides.

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us customize your experience, provide relevant travel tips and offers, and analyze website performance. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you have any questions or requests about your privacy, please contact us.
